About Neptune

NEPTUNE is a leading english medium coaching center in Dhaka. All of our teachers are from english medium background and highly experienced.

NEPTUNE provide the best way and effective methods that contain the art how to control stress, manage pressure and time. We provide competitive environment to students so that they can be well prepared for their exams. Our courses are modified as per student’s requirement. Our purpose is to attract and nurture talent, give them best opportunities for career advancement.

We offer coaching from grade 9 to grade 12 for Cambridge curriculum, both GCE and IGCSE. is popular for its good educational environment and brilliant results every year. It is the oldest complete Cambridge coaching in Dhaka. We provide lab facilities for science subjects as well.

More than 500 students get coaching from NEPTUNE every year. Teachers here highly experienced, expert and well trained in class teaching. They have good educational and professional background. We are determined to do better in future.
